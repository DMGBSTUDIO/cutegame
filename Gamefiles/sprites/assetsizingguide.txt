(hudexampleimages) 

These are simply reference images to help you create the toolbar, or we can 
use the images here they all are 32x32. These 3 images would be placed side by 
side to create a toolbar that is shown at the center bottom of the screen. The 
first shows a pickaxe, the second a hatchet, and the 3rd a sword. Each one can 
be equipped via the number shown on the hud image pressing 1 equips the pickaxe, 

pressing 2 equips the hatchet and pressing 3 equips the sword.
